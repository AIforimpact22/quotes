Scope & Modules

Patient Portal (Authenticated Users)

  • Secure sign-up/login, profile, treatment history, appointments, and reminders.
  • Document uploads (x-rays, reports) and consent records.

Doctor Backend (with Dental Inventory)

  • Dental charting, treatment plans, prescriptions.
  • Inventory of dental supplies (consumables & instruments), low-stock alerts, usage logs.

Assistant Backend

  • Appointment scheduling, chair/room assignments, patient intake, task lists.
  • Queue management & check-in.

Lab Backend

  • Lab case creation, tracking (status, due dates), attachments, delivery records.
  • Integration with doctor & assistant workflows.

Website Interface (Visibility)

  • Modern public website with services, team, and booking entry points.
  • Good visibility practices: clean structure, meta tags, performance, and analytics.

CEO Dashboard (Finance & Resources)

  • Revenue, costs, profitability, utilization, resource allocation reports.
  • CSV/Excel export, period comparisons, role-based access.

Cloud Database & Domain

  • Central cloud database with daily backups and SSL.
  • Custom domain & hosting included.
