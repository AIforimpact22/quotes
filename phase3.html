Scope & Modules

Holdings Backend (Separate from Finance)

  • Company holdings ledger: shares/cap table, fiscal overview, budget tracking, and inventory as an asset class.
  • Governance, audit trails, and cross-links to Finance & Inventory modules for consolidated reporting.

LLM Backend (AI Layer)

  • Conversational interface for founders: insights, summaries, proactive alerts.
  • Connects to holdings, finance, inventory, supplier, and HR data for context-aware answers.

Supplier Backend

  • PO lifecycle, invoices, delivery tracking; integrates with Automated PO logic.
  • Supplier accounts, terms, and performance analytics.

Customer Backend

  • Profiles, loyalty & credit, purchase history; CRM foundation.
  • Personalization signals for LLM and analytics.

Bakery Integration

  • Ingredient usage, production, waste tracking, real-time sales syncing.
  • Links to inventory and supplier ordering to reduce stockouts.

Butchery Integration

  • Batch tracking, yield & waste, costing, and sales synchronization.
  • Improves stock precision and profitability analytics.

Enhancements (Targeted Upgrades)

  • Price Backend: price catalogs, rules, promotions, margin guardrails, and pricing analytics (TBD budget).
  • Data Room: more metrics, dashboards; double analysis depth.
  • Cashier: founder/role-aware balances, credits, KPIs, anomaly alerts.
  • Automated PO: refined forecasting signals, reorder rules, approvals.
  • HR Monitoring: attendance & shift analytics; insights only (no auto decisions).
