1) Equity & Control

  • 15% ordinary shares to Investor at closing.
  • Vesting: 24 months from 1 Jan 2026 (reverse vesting), 3-month cliff.
  • Governance: 1 share = 1 vote. Founder remains statutory director and runs day-to-day.

2) Funding (0% shareholder loan)

  • Instrument: 0% interest shareholder loan, unsecured & subordinated; draw as needed; repay when cash allows.

Covers

  • All legal/notary/lawyer fees for share issue/transfer, trade/statutory name change, and related filings (no cap, paid by Investor).
  • Monthly ops (rent, servers/APIs, domains) — guideline up to €500/month; above by mutual approval.
  • One standard DGA salary for the Founder (incl. employer on-costs) for 12 months once payroll starts.

3) Roles & Light Commitments

  • Founder: product, tech, operations.
  • Investor: sales/strategy support; board-observer rights.
  • Light KPI: e.g., 1 warm intro/month or 1 qualified lead/quarter.

4) Product Milestone (Your Give-Back)

AI4Impact will develop the “LLM Business Analysis” product within 6 months of 1 Jan 2026 — target 30 June 2026 — and prepare it for sale (v1 ready, pricing, landing page, demo deck, onboarding docs, basic T&Cs/privacy).

5) Exits & Transfers

  • 24-month lock-up on transfers.
  • Afterward: ROFR (Right of First Refusal), tag-along for Investor, drag-along at 75% approval.
  • Unvested shares buy-backable at nominal value if contribution stops during vesting; vested at fair-market value (independent valuer if needed).

6) Documents

  • Shareholders’ Agreement (SHA) and Loan Agreement (0% terms, drawdowns, subordination, repayment).
  • Notarial deed for share issue/transfer; update shareholder register.
  • KVK trade name filing “AI4Impact” now; legal name change later if desired.

Note: Non-binding summary. Final terms will be set in the SHA and Loan Agreement (Dutch law).

Scope & Modules

Holdings Backend (Separate from Finance)

  • Schedule: Jan 1–Jan 31, 2026 (build, QA, deploy).
  • Company holdings ledger: shares/cap table, fiscal overview, budget tracking, and inventory as an asset class.
  • Governance, audit trails, and cross-links to Finance & Inventory modules for consolidated reporting.

HR Employee Backend

  • Employee profiles & organizational roster; role/permission management.
  • Attendance & shift logs; monitoring dashboards (insights only, no auto decisions).

LLM Backend (AI Layer)

  • Conversational interface for founders: insights, summaries, proactive alerts.
  • Connects to holdings, finance, inventory, supplier, and HR data for context-aware answers.

Supplier Backend

  • PO lifecycle, invoices, delivery tracking; integrates with Automated PO logic.
  • Supplier accounts, terms, and performance analytics.

Customer Backend

  • Profiles, loyalty & credit, purchase history; CRM foundation.
  • Personalization signals for LLM and analytics.

Bakery Integration

  • Ingredient usage, production, waste tracking, real-time sales syncing.
  • Links to inventory and supplier ordering to reduce stockouts.

Butchery Integration

  • Batch tracking, yield & waste, costing, and sales synchronization.
  • Improves stock precision and profitability analytics.

Enhancements (Targeted Upgrades)

  • Price Backend: price catalogs, rules, promotions, margin guardrails, and pricing analytics (TBD budget).
  • Data Room: more metrics, dashboards; double analysis depth.
  • Cashier: founder/role-aware balances, credits, KPIs, anomaly alerts.
  • Automated PO: refined forecasting signals, reorder rules, approvals.
