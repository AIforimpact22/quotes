ROFR • Tag‑along • Drag‑along 75% Share transfer rules
  • ROFR: Must offer shares to current holders first at the same price/terms.
  • Tag‑along: If majority sells, minority can sell on the same terms (protects minorities).

Example: Founder accepts a buyer for 60%. Investor (15%) can “tag” for 15% at the same price; if 80% approve, they can “drag” everyone to close.
