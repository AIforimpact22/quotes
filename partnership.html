Key Terms

1) Equity & Control

  • Investor receives 15% ordinary shares at closing.
  • Voting: 1 share = 1 vote; Founder remains director with day-to-day control.

2) Vesting (keeps it fair)

  • Investor’s 15% vests over 24 months with a 3-month cliff.
  • If contribution stops during vesting, unvested shares are bought back at nominal value.

3) Funding (simple, no interest)

  • Investor provides a €10,000 shareholder loan, 0% interest, subordinated, drawn as needed.
  • Repay when cash allows; no fixed maturity.

4) What Costs This Covers

  • Monthly ops (rent, servers, APIs, domains) up to €500/month auto-approved.
  • One-off items (e.g., notary; trade/legal name change) up to €1,500 with quick email OK.
  • Anything larger: both approve.

5) Roles & Light Commitments

  • Founder: builds product, runs the company.
  • Investor: sales/strategy support + board-observer rights; light KPI (e.g., 1 warm intro/month or 1 qualified lead/quarter).

6) Exits & Transfers (protect both sides)

  • 24-month lock-up; afterwards: Right of First Refusal on any sale.
  • Tag-along for investor; drag-along at 75% shareholder approval.
  • After vesting, if someone wants out: fair-market-value buy/sell; independent valuer if no agreement.

7) IP & Brand

  • All code/IP belong to AI4Impact B.V.
  • Trade name “AI4Impact” used now; legal name change can follow later.

Documents to Sign

  • Shareholders’ Agreement (SHA) and Loan Agreement.
  • Notary deed for share issue/transfer; update shareholder register.
